1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is designed to be transparent, honest, and compliant with applicable laws including GDPR and CCPA.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 What We Actually Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address (if you contact us)
  • Inquiries: Questions or messages you send to us
  • Newsletter Sign-ups: Email address if you subscribe

2.2 What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or process:

  • Health or medical information
  • Financial information
  • Government ID numbers
  • Date of birth
  • Precise location data
  • Biometric data
  • Sensitive personal information

2.3 Information Collected Automatically (By Google/Third Parties)

Our website uses third-party services (like Google) that may automatically collect: IP Address, Browser Type, Device Information, Pages Visited, and Time Spent on Pages.

Important: We do not have access to this data. It is collected and controlled entirely by Google and other third-party service providers. We only see anonymized aggregate statistics (e.g., "10,000 people visited today").

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Respond to Inquiries: Reply to messages you send us
  • Send Newsletters: If you subscribe (with your consent)
  • Improve Content: Understand what topics interest our readers

3.2 Information Collected by Third Parties

  • Analytics: Understand general traffic patterns
  • Advertising: Serve relevant ads (Google Ads)
  • Performance: Monitor website speed and functionality

We do not use your information for medical diagnosis, treatment, or any healthcare-related decisions.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing, nor do we transfer your data to any external organizations.

5.1 Information You Provide

6. Your Rights & Limitations

6.1 Rights We CAN Fulfill

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of information you provided to us
  • Right to Rectification: Correct information you provided
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of information you provided
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of newsletters or communications

6.2 Rights We CANNOT Fulfill

If you wish to delete data collected by Google, please contact Google directly using their privacy tools: Google Privacy Controls or the Google Analytics Opt-Out.
